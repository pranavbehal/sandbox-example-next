# Product Requirements Document: Soccer Club Ticketing Website

## 1. Introduction
This document outlines the requirements for a new online ticketing website for a soccer club. The primary goal of this platform is to provide a seamless and efficient experience for fans to purchase match tickets, season passes, and potentially other club-related events.

## 2. Goals
*   **Enhance Fan Experience:** Provide a user-friendly and intuitive platform for ticket purchasing.
*   **Increase Ticket Sales Efficiency:** Streamline the ticket sales process, reducing manual overhead.
*   **Improve Data Management:** Centralize customer and sales data for better insights and marketing.
*   **Reduce Queues/Wait Times:** Offer a convenient alternative to physical ticket counters.
*   **Boost Engagement:** Provide a dedicated platform for fans to interact with the club's ticketing services.

## 3. Audience
*   **General Public/Fans:** Individuals looking to purchase tickets for single matches.
*   **Season Ticket Holders:** Existing holders needing to renew or manage their passes.
*   **Club Members:** Members eligible for special discounts or early access.
*   **Club Administration:** Staff responsible for managing events, ticket inventory, and customer support.

## 4. Features

### 4.1. User Management
*   **User Registration & Login:** Secure account creation and authentication.
*   **Profile Management:** Users can update personal information, password, and communication preferences.
*   **Purchase History:** Users can view past ticket purchases and order details.

### 4.2. Event & Ticket Management
*   **Event Listing:** Display upcoming matches and other club events (e.g., stadium tours, fan events).
*   **Seat Selection:** Interactive stadium map for seat selection (if applicable, for allocated seating).
*   **Ticket Types:** Support for various ticket types (e.g., adult, child, concession, VIP).
*   **Pricing & Discounts:** Dynamic pricing based on opponent, date, and seating category; application of promotional codes and member discounts.
*   **Availability Display:** Real-time updates on ticket availability.

### 4.3. Shopping Cart & Checkout
*   **Shopping Cart:** Users can add multiple tickets/items before checkout.
*   **Secure Payment Gateway:** Integration with secure payment providers (e.g., credit/debit card, mobile payments).
*   **Order Confirmation:** Email confirmation with e-tickets (PDF, QR code) and transaction details.

### 4.4. Administration Panel
*   **Event Creation & Management:** Admins can add, edit, and delete events, set dates, times, and venues.
*   **Ticket Inventory Management:** Manage ticket allocations for different sections and events.
*   **User Management:** View and manage user accounts.
*   **Sales Reporting:** Generate reports on ticket sales, revenue, and attendance.
*   **Discount Code Management:** Create and manage promotional codes.

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

### 5.1. Performance
*   **Scalability:** Ability to handle high traffic during peak sale periods (e.g., derby matches).
*   **Response Time:** Pages should load within 2-3 seconds under normal load.

### 5.2. Security
*   **Data Encryption:** All sensitive data (payment info, personal details) must be encrypted.
*   **Authentication & Authorization:** Robust user authentication and role-based access control for admins.
*   **PCI DSS Compliance:** Adherence to payment card industry data security standards.

### 5.3. Usability
*   **Intuitive Interface:** Easy to navigate and use for all user demographics.
*   **Mobile Responsiveness:** Optimized for various devices (desktop, tablet, mobile).

### 5.4. Reliability
*   **Uptime:** Target 99.9% uptime.
*   **Error Handling:** Graceful handling of errors and informative messages.

## 6. Future Considerations (Out of Scope for Initial Release)
*   **Merchandise Integration:** Link to club shop for merchandise sales.
*   **Fan Community Features:** Forums, polls, or news updates.
*   **Multi-language Support:** For international fan bases.
*   **API for Third-Party Integrations:** For partners or resellers.
*   **Refunds and Exchanges:** Process for managing ticket refunds and exchanges.
