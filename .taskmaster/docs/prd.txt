# Product Requirements Document: Soccer Club Ticketing Website

## 1. Introduction

### Purpose
This Product Requirements Document (PRD) outlines the features, functionality, and requirements for a new online ticketing website for a soccer club. The aim is to provide a seamless and efficient platform for fans to purchase match tickets and for the club to manage ticketing operations.

### Scope
This project encompasses the development of a web-based application that allows users to browse upcoming matches, select and purchase tickets, and manage their bookings. It also includes an administrative interface for the club to manage events, ticket inventory, and view sales data.
Out of scope for this initial phase are native mobile applications, stadium access control integrations, and complex loyalty program integrations beyond basic fan accounts.

## 2. Goals

### Business Goals
*   Increase overall ticket sales and attendance for home matches.
*   Enhance the fan experience by providing an easy-to-use and reliable ticketing platform.
*   Streamline ticket management processes for club administrators.
*   Reduce operational costs associated with manual ticket sales.
*   Improve data collection on fan demographics and purchasing habits.

### User Goals
*   Fans can easily find information about upcoming matches.
*   Fans can quickly and securely purchase tickets for desired matches.
*   Fans can access their purchased tickets conveniently (e.g., via email or mobile).
*   Fans can view their past purchase history.
*   Club administrators can efficiently create and manage match events and ticket allocations.
*   Club administrators can monitor ticket sales and generate reports.

## 3. User Stories/Personas

### Fan Persona: Avid Supporter Amy
Amy is a loyal fan who attends most home games. She values convenience and speed when buying tickets. She wants to be able to:
*   Browse upcoming matches and see available seating.
*   Select her preferred seats and quantity of tickets.
*   Complete her purchase quickly and securely using her preferred payment method.
*   Receive her tickets promptly via email or a mobile pass.
*   View her purchase history and manage her account details.

### Club Administrator Persona: Operations Manager Mark
Mark is responsible for managing match day operations and ticketing. He needs a system that allows him to:
*   Create new match events with details like date, time, opponent, and venue.
*   Define seating sections, ticket categories (e.g., adult, child), and pricing.
*   Allocate ticket inventory for each match and section.
*   Track real-time ticket sales and availability.
*   Generate reports on sales, attendance, and revenue.
*   Manage user accounts and address ticketing inquiries.

## 4. Features

### Core User-Facing Functionality
*   **Event Listing & Details:**
    *   Display upcoming soccer matches with date, time, opponent, and venue.
    *   Detailed match pages with stadium map, available sections, and ticket categories.
*   **Ticket Selection & Purchase:**
    *   Interactive seat selection (if applicable, otherwise section-based).
    *   Quantity selection for tickets.
    *   Shopping cart functionality.
    *   Secure checkout process.
*   **User Account Management:**
    *   User registration and login.
    *   Profile management (personal details, saved payment methods).
    *   Purchase history viewing.
*   **Payment Gateway Integration:**
    *   Support for major credit/debit cards.
    *   Integration with a secure payment gateway (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
*   **Ticket Delivery:**
    *   Email delivery of e-tickets (PDF or scannable QR code).
    *   Option for mobile wallet integration (Apple Wallet, Google Pay) for tickets.

### Admin Panel Functionality
*   **Event Management:**
    *   Create, edit, and delete match events.
    *   Configure match details (date, time, opponent, venue).
*   **Ticket Inventory Management:**
    *   Define seating sections, rows, and individual seats (if granular).
    *   Set ticket categories and pricing (e.g., standard, VIP, child).
    *   Manage ticket availability and allocate inventory per match/section.
    *   View real-time ticket sales and remaining inventory.
*   **User Management:**
    *   View and manage registered user accounts.
    *   Search for specific bookings or users.
*   **Sales Reporting:**
    *   Generate reports on ticket sales by match, date range, or ticket category.
    *   Export sales data for further analysis.

### Future Considerations (Nice-to-have)
*   **Season Passes/Memberships:** Functionality to purchase and manage season tickets or club memberships.
*   **Group Bookings:** Dedicated flow for purchasing tickets for large groups.
*   **Promotional Codes:** Ability for administrators to create and manage discount codes.
*   **Fan Loyalty Program Integration:** Link with an existing or future club loyalty program.
*   **Multi-language Support:** Offer the website in multiple languages.

## 5. Technical Requirements (High-level)

*   **Scalability:** The platform must be able to handle peak traffic during popular ticket releases without performance degradation.
*   **Security:** Adherence to PCI DSS compliance for handling payment information. Robust security measures to protect user data and prevent fraud.
*   **Performance:** Fast loading times for all pages, especially during the ticket purchase flow. Efficient database queries for real-time inventory updates.
*   **Reliability:** High uptime and minimal downtime. Redundancy measures for critical components.
*   **Integration:** Potential for future integration with existing club CRM, marketing, or access control systems.
*   **Technology Stack:** (To be determined by the development team, but consider modern, scalable web technologies.)

## 6. Success Metrics

*   **Ticket Sales Volume:** Number of tickets sold per match and per season.
*   **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of website visitors who complete a ticket purchase.
*   **User Satisfaction:** Measured through surveys, feedback, and repeat purchases.
*   **Website Uptime:** Percentage of time the website is accessible and functional.
*   **Page Load Time:** Average time taken for key pages to load.
*   **Administrative Efficiency:** Reduction in time and effort for club staff to manage ticketing.
